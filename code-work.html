work

I have handpicked a couple of different projects I found fun and challenging.

Holiday Service Website

CSS, HTML, Vanilla Javascript, Responsive Design

I've designed and developed a website for a holiday service business. I used classic HTML, CSS and Vanilla Javascript. I chose the colors based on holiday vibes and nature vibes. I have kept the design simple and easy to navigate through. On the front page the user will get the most relevant information first (pricing, routes and caravan to rent) before she can decide to dig deeper and find specific information by navigating from the burger menu.

My Personal Website

Designers Community Landing Page

CSS, HTML

On the 'Besigners' landing page I've played around with background-graphics I made in Figma. For this designer community I wanted to add a fresh expression with purple and light green colors.
