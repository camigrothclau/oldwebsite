about me

I am Camilla

Kid of the eighties (almost nineties) and from the country side up north of Zealand, Denmark. I have, though, lived in Copenhagen for more than ten years now and I’m quite sure that I’ll never get tired of this city. Lovely parks, cafés, bars, breweries, people and perfect distances on bike. And great music venues and festivals!

I just learned myself to code this website with HTML, CSS and Javascript. I’ve always been eager to learn new things. I’m also learning Bootstrap, SASS and planning to learn myself React, Angular and Typescript also. Please let me know if you know a related course or video that I just have to see.

I’ve worked as a User Experience Researcher for 4 years both as a consultant but also as a in-house researcher. I enjoy research because I’m a problem solver by heart, my pre-understandings are challenged and I learn about digital behaviours, opinions attached to human goals within the human-computer-interactions.

I especially enjoy coding and creating simple, creative and colourful designs. After working with various it departments and clever developers and designers I’ve found out that webdesign is a great interest for me and I'm about to do a career change moving into UI/Frontend Design.

When I’m not joggling with tasks within the digital field I produce music, sing in a choir (we’re called Røst, please check us out), dance Chorus Line (a dance from the thirties) and go to festivals with my friends enjoying music and lovely, fresh cold beer.

This is me! Feel free to reach out to me about coding, UX research, design disciplines or a chat about the weather. Take care.
